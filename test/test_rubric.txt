== COMPANY: TechCorp India ==
Industry: B2B SaaS | Stage: Series B | Team Size: 80

== SENIORITY LEVELS ==

Intern (0 years):
- Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree
- Can complete well-scoped tasks with guidance
- Shortlist threshold: 50+

Junior Engineer (0-2 years):
- Knows at least one backend or frontend language well
- Familiar with Git, basic SQL, REST APIs
- Can ship small features with code review
- Shortlist threshold: 55+

Mid Engineer (2-5 years):
- Owns features end-to-end independently
- Strong in one domain (backend/frontend/data)
- Writes clean, testable code
- Shortlist threshold: 65+

Senior Engineer (5-8 years):
- Designs systems, leads technical decisions for a squad
- Mentors 2-3 junior/mid engineers
- Strong in system design, distributed systems
- Shortlist threshold: 70+

Staff Engineer (8+ years):
- Sets technical direction across multiple teams
- Writes RFCs, drives architecture reviews
- Rarely writes code, mostly unblocks others
- Shortlist threshold: 78+

== WHAT WE VALUE ==
- Ownership over activity — we want people who ship, not just plan
- Clear written communication (async-first company)
- Prior startup or product company experience is a strong signal
- Open source contributions are a bonus, not a requirement
- Do NOT penalise for: employment gaps under 18 months, bootcamp education,
  non-English university names, or short stints at failed startups

== INTERVIEW PROCESS ==

All roles:
Round 1 — Take-home assignment (2-3 hours): practical problem relevant to the role
Round 2 — Technical debrief (45 min): walkthrough of take-home, follow-up questions
Round 3 — System design (60 min, Senior+ only): whiteboard a real-world system
Round 4 — Culture + values (30 min): with Engineering Manager or a peer

Interviewers:
- Round 1: async, no interviewer
- Round 2: Senior Engineer from the hiring team
- Round 3: Staff Engineer or CTO
- Round 4: Engineering Manager
